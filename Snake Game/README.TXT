USER INSTRUCTIONS:
1. The purpose of the game is to collect the squares that resemble food
2. Use the keyboard to move and collect the squares
3. Each piece of food eaten adds size and speeds up the snake
4. If you hit yourself or the wall you die and restart
5. Score is saved and recorded
